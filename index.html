About Me

Hello, I am Walker Ashmore and I am a UX/UI designer. I’m currently 21-years-old and I was raised in Atlanta, GA. I have been a student of design and UX/UI for 3 years. I always had an extensive passion for creativity and technology. As I’ve grown in my education, I’ve come to appreciate creative innovation and the problem solving existent within user experiences.

Through my experiences in internships and design education, I have learned that users are the first priority and collaboration/communication are vital to problem solving.

What I enjoy the most about design is being as creative as possible, getting feedback on each specific component during testing, and implementing revisions to establish a perfect solution to aid consumer problems.
